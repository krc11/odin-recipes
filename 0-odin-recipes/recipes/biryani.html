dum biryani

Chicken Biryani is a flavorful South Asian mixed rice dish made with marinated chicken, aromatic basmati rice, and a blend of spices, often cooked in layers using a traditional Persian dum pukht slow-cooking method. It is a beloved and popular dish, especially in India and Pakistan, and is often reserved for special occasions and celebrations.
